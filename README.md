# PIMCO GIS Income Fund — Monthly Tracker

Tracks the **PIMCO GIS Income Fund (E-Class USD Accumulation, ISIN IE00B7KFL990)** on a monthly basis across three areas:

1. **Asset Allocation** (% Gross Market Value) — Government Related, Agency MBS, Securitized, IG Credit, High Yield, Bank Loan, Emerging Markets, Cash & Liquidity, Other.
2. **Key Statistics** — Effective Duration, Estimated Yield to Maturity, YTD Return (net), Average Credit Quality.
3. **Credit Quality** (% Market Value) — AAA → Below B, plus A1/P1 short-term cash equivalents.

## Files
- `PIMCO_GIS_Income_Dashboard.html` — self-contained interactive dashboard (open in any browser). Charts + data tables with month-over-month change highlighting.
- `PIMCO_GIS_Income_Tracker.xlsx` — spreadsheet version with charts, structured for adding future months.

## Data
Figures read from PIMCO GIS Income Fund monthly reports. 13 months with data: Jan–Mar, Jun–Nov 2025 and Jan–Apr 2026 (no published report for Apr-25, May-25, Dec-25).

> **Methodology note:** From June 2025 PIMCO changed its reporting (GMV% now includes interest-rate-swap notional). The dominant credit-quality bucket shifts from AAA (~64%) to AA (~58%) — a reporting change, not a portfolio change. Compare within each period.
